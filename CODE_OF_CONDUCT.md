
# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Shrabanti Bala Joya, Sarisha Das, Omowunmi Obadero, Mantram Sharma

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make our community welcoming, safe, and equitable for all.

We are committed to fostering an environment that respects and promotes the dignity, rights, and contributions of all individuals, regardless of characteristics including race, ethnicity, caste, color, age, physical characteristics, neurodiversity, disability, sex or gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, language, philosophy or religion, national or social origin, socio-economic position, level of education, or other status. The same privileges of participation are extended to everyone who participates in good faith and in accordance with this Covenant.

## Our Standards

Examples of encouraged behaviours:

1. Respecting the community and its purpose of sharing knowlege.
2. Engaging with kindness and empathy.
3. Respecting diverse perspectives and experiences.
4. Taking responsibility for our actions and contributions.
5. Offering and receiving constructive feedback with grace and openness.
6. Make meaningful efforts to repair harm caused by our actions.
7. Acting in ways to support wellbeing and growth of the community.

Examples of unacceptable behaviours:

1. Violating personal boundaries.
2. Making insulting or judgemental comments about an individual or group’s appearance, identity, or personal characteristics.
3. Harrasing others in public or private spaces.
4. Engaging in behavior or communication that is sexually suggestive or inappropriately intimate.
5. Sharing or using someone's personal information without their consent.
6. Encouraging violence and harm to any individual or group.

## Enforcement

To report a possible violation of the Code of Conduct, please email shrabantibala1502@gmail.com. All reports will be handled fairly, confidentially, and reviewed in a timely manner.

Community Moderators will investigate reports with discretion, to ensure transparency where appropriate while prioritizing safety, well being and respect for all involved.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Community Moderators will follow these guidelines when addressing actions that violate this code of conduct.

1) Correction
   1) Event: Use of deragatory language, signs or inappropriate behaviour.
   2) Consequence: A private conversation to address the violation and discuss corrections.
      
3) Warning
   1) Event: A single incident or series of incidents.
   2) Consequence: A written warning outlining the behaviour, its impact and next steps if continues.
      
4) Temporary Ban
   1) Event: Repeated violations which have been addressed before or a single serious violation.
   2) Consequence: A written notice explaining the duration of the temporary ban and expectations for returning to the community.
      
5) Permanent Ban
   1) Event: Ongoing or severe violations that compromise community safety or well-being.
   2) Consequence: A written notice and permanent removal of access from all related community spaces.
      
## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces and activities. It also applies whenever someone represents the community in 
public or online — for example, using an official email address, posting from a community social account, or participating in events as a designated representative.
